# **App Name**: FelixLash Landing

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Elegant hero section with full-screen background and prominent 'Book on WhatsApp' CTA.
- Lash Styles Catalog: A well laid out catalog of lash styles, including a high quality photo, prices for application and maintenance and service duration
- About Rafaela Section: Section showcasing Rafaela's expertise and brand values, and providing a button to book.
- Pricing Section: Clearly visible, stylish presentation of pricing information.
- Contact and Location: A animated contact card with all location and contact info, as well as floating Reserve button.
- Photo Enhancement: Use an AI tool to modify provided pictures to fit into a consistent theme and aesthetic. Use style transfer and color palette adjustments as needed. Also make adjustments such as blur/sharpness automatically as needed, and crop/scale/rotate as needed.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blush pink (#F2D7D9) for a feminine and romantic feel.
- Background color: Off-white (#FAF9F6) to provide a clean, luxurious backdrop.
- Accent color: Rose gold (#B76E79) used sparingly for highlights and CTAs to add a touch of elegance.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif, for elegant headlines.
- Body font: 'Poppins', sans-serif, for body text and CTAs to ensure readability. 
- Use thin-line icons from Heroicons or Lucide for a modern and delicate feel.
- Subtle fade-in and zoom animations on images and sections to enhance user engagement.
- Mobile-first design with a sticky header and a prominent floating WhatsApp CTA.
